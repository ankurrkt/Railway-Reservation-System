Kerala
Kerala is known as “God's Own Country and is home to a variety of attractions that cater to different interests. Kerala is famous for its backwaters, which are a network of lagoons, lakes, and canals that run parallel to the Arabian Sea.
Jaipur
Jaipur is a city that is filled with vibrant colors, stunning art and ancient artifacts and sculptures that are among the most famous features of the city in pink.
Sikkim
Sikkim is known for its pristine natural beauty, vibrant Buddhist monasteries and adventure activities. Sikkim is home to mighty Himalayas and has around 600 species of orchids, 240 species of trees and ferns, 150 varieties of gladioli, 46 types of rhododendrons along with a variety of magnolias and many other foliage plants
Shimla
Shimla, also known as the “Queen of Hills, is a hub for adventure activities such as trekking, camping, rock climbing, and paragliding. The town is home to several beautiful parks, gardens, and nature trails that offer breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape.
Agra
Agra is home to several historical monuments and landmarks that are worth visiting like Taj Mahal and Fatehpur Sikri. It is also known for its delicious Mughlai cuisine, which is a blend of Indian and Persian flavors
Goa
Goa is known for its mesmerizing beaches, alluring forts, picturesque sceneries, charming festivals, delicious food, spirituality, shopping, activities, nightlife, religious places, romance, water sports, Portuguese culture, accommodations for all budgets, beautiful wildlife, museums, flea markets, and opportunities for volunteering.
