Service at Station

Information Desk

Get assistance and information about trains, schedules, and station facilities.

Waiting Lounge

Relax in a comfortable lounge area while waiting for your train.

Restrooms

Clean and well-maintained restroom facilities are available.

Drinking water

Drinking water is available at most stations. It is provided through water coolers or water dispensers.

Food stalls

Food stalls are available at most stations. They offer a variety of snacks, beverages, and meals.

Parking

Parking facilities are available at most stations. They are usually located near the entrance of the station.
